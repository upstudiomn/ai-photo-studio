# Business Plan

## Business category
AI photo service + ecommerce + local print fulfillment.

## Current implementation status

- Preview-first customer journey is implemented in the app and tested in local E2E.
- Manual payment and admin fulfillment are the current MVP approach.
- Digital, print, and combined product choices create confirmed order records only after checkout.
- Production AI quality, QPay automation, and launch/legal pages remain planned.

## Main revenue streams
1. Digital AI image downloads
2. A4/A3 printed photo/poster orders
3. Premium framed or gift print packages
4. Manual retouching premium service
5. Business product photo enhancement
6. Seasonal gift campaigns

## Target customers
| Segment | Product |
|---|---|
| Families | Old photo restore, family merge, framed gift |
| Parents | Kids poster, birthday print, storybook portrait |
| Couples | Couple cinematic portrait, anniversary print |
| Pet owners | Pet portrait |
| Small businesses | Product photo background upgrade |
| Graduates/professionals | AI studio profile portrait |
| Gift buyers | A4/A3 premium print package |

## Best first product
Family Memory Pack:

- User uploads 2–3 separate photos
- AI creates one premium family portrait
- Admin checks quality
- Customer orders A3 print or framed gift
- Local delivery in Mongolia

## Why this can work in Mongolia
- People value family memories and printed gifts.
- Many people have old damaged family photos.
- Many families do not have a good photo together.
- Local printing and delivery creates more trust than digital-only AI service.
- Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok before/after content can sell strongly.

## Business positioning
Do not sell this as “AI prompt generation.”
Sell it as:

> Premium AI memory photos with local print delivery.

## First launch offer
- Old photo restore digital
- Colorize + restore
- AI studio portrait
- A4/A3 print add-on
- Limited family merge beta with admin review
