# Pricing Plan

## Principle
Do not price based only on AI cost. Price based on emotional value, print quality, convenience, and local delivery.

## Current implementation status

- The app currently supports digital, print, and combined product choices after preview results.
- Product/order records are created only during checkout.
- Prices are MVP/static and should be validated before launch.
- QPay/payment automation is planned; current payment records are manual/pending.
